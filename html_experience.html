Work at Burger King

After filling out the form, I was called for an interview session. I am grateful for passing and getting a job as a part time cashier. In a day I work for 6 hours. I got a good friend who taught me with patience. A week before the semester started, I quit my job. That was my experience while working at Burger King.This is an experience that I won't forget til my last breath.
